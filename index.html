HOW DOES A SOLAR PV SYSTEM WORK?

A Solar PV System works in mysterious ways! Not really Let us explain:-

  • The Sun Shines Bright Causing The Photovoltaic Cells Of A Solar Pv System To Heat Up!
  • The Contact Between The Rays And The Photovoltaic Cells Creates A Direct Current.
  • An Inverter Converts This Direct Current Into Alternating Current.
  • This Current Subsequently Reaches The Predestined Location Through A Switchboard And Voila! You Have Light!
  • If There Is An Excess Of Energy Generated, It Is Either Stored In A Battery For Future Use Or Sent To The Electricity Grid For Others To Use.
  • This Excess Energy Sent To The Grid Is Credited To The Next Electricity Bill Saving A Lot Of Money!
